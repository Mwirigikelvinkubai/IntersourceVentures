Intersource Ventures Limited

A Kenyan company specialising in the importation, distribution, and wholesale supply of quality food supplements and baby essentials — connecting global manufacturers with local retailers, pharmacies, and institutions.

Our mission

To make quality, affordable wellness and baby products accessible across Kenya — delivered reliably, ethically, and at competitive wholesale prices that work for our partners and their customers.

"We Care"

  • Competitive wholesale pricing
  • Reliable, on-time delivery
  • Ethical and compliant sourcing
  • Customer-first service
