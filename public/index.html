About us

Importers. Distributors. Wholesalers.

Intersource Ventures Limited is a Kenyan importer and distributor of quality wellness products and baby essentials. We source food supplements and baby-care lines directly from trusted international manufacturers, then supply pharmacies, supermarkets, baby shops and wellness retailers nationwide — reliably and at fair wholesale prices.

Our Mission
To make quality wellness and baby products dependably available to every Kenyan retailer.
Our Vision
To be East Africa's most trusted wellness & baby-products distribution partner.
Genuine sourcing
Every SKU traceable to its manufacturer of record.
Retailer-first
Volume discounts, fast restocks, dedicated account support.
